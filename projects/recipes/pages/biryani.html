Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish with its origins among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and/or vegetables such as potatoes in certain regional varieties. Biryani is generally spicy and can be mildly to very hot, according to individual taste. It is usually served with a raita or cucumber salad and a sweet dessert. Biryani is a popular dish throughout the Indian subcontinent and among the diaspora from the region. It is one of the most popular rice dishes in the world.
